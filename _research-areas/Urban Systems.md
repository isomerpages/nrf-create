<p>The Urban Systems (i.e. energy and environmental sustainability, urban
mobility, and urban design and territorial planning) is one out of four
interdisciplinary thematic areas of research for CREATE that was chosen
to be part of an overarching theme of system of systems. The following
research centres carry out research in the field of Urban Systems:</p>
